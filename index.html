Quick Rest Ideas
Start one morning without an alarm and ease into the day.
Make a warm drink and enjoy without multitasking.
Do a small rest task (clean your desk, room, or photo gallery).
Try something creative; sketch, cook, build, write, or have a gaming session.
Catch up with someone you haven’t talked to in a while or a hometown friend.
Give yourself permission to do nothing for a bit.
